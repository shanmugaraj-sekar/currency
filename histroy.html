Dollar

The dollar is the official currency of several countries, including the United States, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and Singapore, among others. It is a widely recognized and accepted currency around the world, often used in international trade and finance. The dollar is subdivided into 100 smaller units, such as cents or pennies, depending on the country. The value of the dollar can fluctuate based on various economic and geopolitical factors, and its exchange rate is often closely watched by governments, businesses, and investors. Overall, the dollar plays a significant role in the global economy and is a key factor in many financial transactions and investments.
